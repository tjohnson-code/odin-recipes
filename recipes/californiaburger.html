California Burger

Description

This is a Midwest take on a California Burger. If you're feeling more adventurous feel free to add slices of avocado or a spread of guacamole.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. Ground Beef 80/20
  • Sea Salt to Taste
  • 1 tsp. Black Pepper
  • American Cheese (or cheese of your choice)
  • Vine-Ripened Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Pickle Slices(on the side if preferred)
  • Mayo
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Buns

Directions

  1. Season ground beef with salt & pepper
  2. Create bun sized patties out of the hamburger
  3. Cook/grill burgers - approximately 4 minutes before flipping
  4. Flip burgers
  5. Add cheese about a minute before completion
  6. Burgers are done once they reach 140 degrees (medium)
  7. Let rest
  8. Top your burger however you like! A true California Burger requires the tomato, lettuce, mayo, and ketchup at the very least
  9. ENJOY!
